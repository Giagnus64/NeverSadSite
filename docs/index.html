About Us

Nehemiah and Jed are writers, comedians, and biological brothers based in New York City. Together they comprise the comedy pair, Never Sad.


Their original one-act scene was performed at the ABC Discovers Talent Showcase in 2016, they were finalists in Universal Cable Productions' 2017 Pitch Fest, and a series they're currently developing has progressed to the final round of the 2018 Sundance Institute | YouTube New Voices Lab.


Nehemiah and Jed host the Always Happy Video Comedy Bop, which has been featured in The New York Times. They frequently contribute to The New Yorker, Funny or Die, and Youtube.
